# Public repository policy

## Purpose

The repository is a testable proof of capability for industrial data science and AI engineering.

## Required public qualities

- one-command setup;
- green continuous integration;
- real public data;
- reproducible outputs;
- explicit assumptions;
- no proprietary company information;
- no employer logos or implied endorsement;
- no fabricated savings, model performance, or operational constraints;
- no credentials, tokens, personal documents, or application correspondence.

## Publication timing

Develop privately or locally until the first end-to-end baseline is credible. Switch to public before outreach, but only after CI passes and the repository produces a real brief. An unfinished public notebook dump reduces perceived value.
